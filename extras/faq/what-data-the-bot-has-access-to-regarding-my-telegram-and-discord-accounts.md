# What data the bot has access to regarding my Telegram and Discord accounts?

Thank you for your interest in our message forwarding bot from Discord to Telegram.

### ✅ What data does the bot access?

The bot only accesses the necessary data to perform message forwarding, and only in the channels/groups you’ve authorized:\
• On Telegram, the bot can read and send messages only in the groups or channels where you’ve explicitly added it and granted the appropriate permissions (e.g., read and send messages).\
• On Discord, the bot receives messages only from the channels you configure for forwarding.

### 🔐 Privacy and Security:

We ensure that:\
• The bot does not collect any personal data from your Telegram or Discord accounts.\
• All access is strictly limited to the technical operation of the forwarding function.\
• No data is shared with third parties, and you can request deletion of any data at any time.
