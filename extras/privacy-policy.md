# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy for D2T Auto Forward Bot**

#### 1. Introduction

Welcome to D2T Auto Forward Bot. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our service.

#### 2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data. However, the app may process the following data for functionality:

* **User-provided settings:** Configuration data required to forward messages between platforms.
* **Message forwarding data:** Temporary processing of messages for transmission from Discord to Telegram.
* **Log data:** May include basic information such as timestamps and error logs to improve the app’s functionality.

#### 3. How We Use Information

We only process data to enable the core functionality of the bot, which includes:

* Forwarding messages between Discord and Telegram based on user-defined settings.
* Improving app performance and troubleshooting technical issues.
* Ensuring compliance with relevant policies.

#### 4. Data Retention & Security

We do not store forwarded messages or personal data permanently. Any temporary processing is handled securely, and data is not shared with third parties.

#### 5. Third-Party Services

D2T Auto Forward Bot interacts with third-party platforms such as Discord and Telegram. We do not control these services, and you should refer to their privacy policies:

* [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
* [Telegram Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy)

#### 6. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

* Configure and disable the bot at any time.
* Delete or modify forwarded content based on platform settings.

#### 7. Compliance with Google Play & Apple App Store Policies

This application strictly adheres to Google Play and Apple App Store policies by:

* Not collecting personal information without user consent.
* Not engaging in spam or unauthorized automation.
* Ensuring compliance with the data protection guidelines of third-party services.

#### 8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app or our official website.
