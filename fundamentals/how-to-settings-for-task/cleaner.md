#### **Overview**

* **Purpose**: The **Cleaner** tool allows you to automatically remove specific content types from messages that match the defined conditions.
* **Instructions**: Toggle the cleaner for each content type:
  * **✓ (Green)**: Activates the cleaner to remove this content type.
  * **✕ (Gray)**: Deactivates the cleaner, leaving this content type untouched.

***

#### **Cleaner Types**

* **CLEANER URL**: Removes URLs from messages.
* **CLEANER URL PREVIEW**: Removes preview metadata from shared URLs.
* **CLEANER HASHTAG**: Removes hashtags (`#example`) from messages.
* **CLEANER MENTION**: Removes mentions of users (e.g., `@username`) from messages.
* **CLEANER AUDIO**: Removes audio files from messages.
* **CLEANER VIDEO**: Removes video files from messages.
* **CLEANER PHOTO**: Removes images from messages.
* **CLEANER STICKER**: Removes stickers from messages.
* **CLEANER TEXT**: Removes text content from messages.
* **CLEANER DOCUMENT**: Removes documents or files from messages.
* **CLEANER VIDEO NOTE**: Removes video notes from messages.
* **CLEANER VOICE**: Removes voice messages.
* **CLEANER PHOTO WITHOUT TEXT**: Removes photos without accompanying text.
* **CLEANER DICE**: Removes dice-rolling animations or similar interactive elements.
