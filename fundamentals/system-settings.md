#### **System Settings Overview**

This screen provides system settings and options to manage the bot's configuration and access support. Here's the breakdown:

***

#### **Bot Configuration**

1. **BOT Active State**:
   * Displays the current status of the bot (**Running** in this case).
   * Toggle the switch to enable or disable bot operations.
2. **Restart Bot**:
   * Restart the bot to apply updates or resolve any operational issues.
3. **Config Token**:
   * Update or change the Discord or Telegram token associated with the bot.

***

#### **Support or Report Issue**

1. **Reach Customer Support**:
   * Contact the support team directly for assistance or to report any issues.
2. **Join the News Channel**:
   * Subscribe to the bot’s news or updates channel for the latest information.

***

#### **Use Case:**

This menu is designed to provide full control over bot functionality and quick access to support and updates, ensuring seamless operations and troubleshooting when needed. Let me know if you need further explanations!
